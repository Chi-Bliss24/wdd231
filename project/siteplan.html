Wale Street Commerce

Empowering small businesses through digital trade

Site Name

Name: Wale Street Commerce

This name represents a digital marketplace inspired by the energy and diversity of Wale Street — a symbol of commerce and culture. It conveys a place where entrepreneurs, artisans, and shoppers meet to buy, sell, and connect online.

Optional domain availability: walestreetcommerce.com

Site Purpose

The purpose of Wale Street Commerce is to provide a simple and accessible e-commerce platform where small business owners and local vendors can showcase their products to a wider audience. The site will feature vendor profiles, product listings, a secure checkout system, and business resources to help users grow their online presence.

Scenarios

  • How can I set up a shop and start selling my handmade products online?
  • Where can I browse local vendors and discover new products from small businesses?
  • What payment options and shipping methods are supported on Wale Street Commerce?

Color Scheme

Primary Color: #2A9D8F — Used for headings, buttons, and accents.

Secondary Color: #E9C46A — Used for highlights and backgrounds.

The color palette is inspired by balance and trust — teal for reliability and yellow-gold for energy and optimism.

Typography

Heading Font: "Poppins", sans-serif — used for all headers.

Body Font: "Open Sans", sans-serif — used for main content and paragraphs.

The combination provides a modern, clean look that maintains readability across devices.
